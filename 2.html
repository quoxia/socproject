Beliefs and Values

  • Nettles had a lot of knowledge of the Bible, and thought that she and Applewhite were the "chosen two" from the Book of Revelation.
  • The two, personally, thought that their "calling" meant that they didn't have to follow laws...
  • They thought that their bodies were just containers or vehicles for holy beings to live in for a while.
  • There were strange diets that members were required to follow sometimes.
  • Other activites, like sex and smoking and drinking, were forbidden.
  • Members were required to wear baggy clothing and keep short hair.
  • Applewhite thought that the Hale-Bopp Comet was a sign of a spaceship coming to get them and take them to the "Next Level."
  • When the comet came closest to Earth, the members of the cult killed themselves by drinking a mixture of vodka and some lethal drugs.
