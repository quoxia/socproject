Who was in this Cult?

  • Despite what one may expect, many of the people in the cult were just normal people
  • Nettles and Applewhite would advertise their cult, and people who were curious would look into it for a chance to learn the meaning of life
  • Preaching the knowledge of what happens after death and the meaning of life is a surefire way to gain followers, and that is just what Nettles and Applewhite did.
