Background Information

  • Leaders: Bonnie Lu Nettles (AKA "Ti") and Marshall Applewhite (AKA "Do")
  • Applewhite almost died and Nettles was his nurse in 1972. Nettles convinced Applewhite to join her in her beliefs.
  • Applewhite went on after Nettles' death to lead the cult.
  • Applewhite was born in Spur, Texas on May 17, 1931.
  • He led a normal life before his near-death experience.
  • Two years before he met Nettles, he divorced a woman with whom he had two children, and there were rumors that he had trouble with his sexual identity. This caused stress in his life.
  • As stated, the two met in the hospital and it all spiraled from there.
